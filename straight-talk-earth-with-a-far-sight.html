Earth Viewed With a Far Sight

The bright side is, if we keep focus on how the data gathering & expressive aptitudes of the Cyber are syncing up with the withering sustenance of our HeroStories, then Cyber’s view, as if from a great height, has a chance to reveal our collective humanity; might give an opportunity for a distanced view of ourselves and earth, the aim of our yearning explorations since the cave.

Our quest, if you like.

Here, Cyber is giving us a statistical truth of the limits of earth’s resources; our tantrum child’s waste of which being, now, an even bigger threat than any OtherClan over there in that next valley…

Should we choose to accept it.

We come to understand this earthly home and its generosities that sustain us are finite. Cyber’s all-encompassing-ness lays out before us, literally, the ends of the Earth.

I know, it’s a hard look, directly at that melt…
