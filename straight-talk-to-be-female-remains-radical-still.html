Re: To Be Female Remains Radical, Still

I might as well tell you, straight out, I believe being female in this world remains radical, still.

By radical I mean socially in-consumable in fundamental ways that, for example, the counter culture or other creative impulses toward human opening have proved not to be.

Brushfires...

These cultural resources, at first bristling with energy are time and again depleted, their potentially life sustaining meaning siphoned off into the addicted & monetized purposes of commerce & capital - "The Sell."

I believe the female is at root in-consumable because human life, defined both literally and as the transmutational essence at our core, cannot come into being without us.

Such a long and luminous arc of heart.

WeWomen of a primary position in the biological genetic sequence, therefore we are a keystone in the social genome. A change in us changes all.
